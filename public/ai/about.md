# Propel-Lab LLC — Company Overview

## About

Propel-Lab LLC (合同会社 Propel-Lab) is an AI/LLM technology company based in Fukuoka, Japan, founded in April 2025. We engineer next-generation business models through systems thinking and AI technology.
